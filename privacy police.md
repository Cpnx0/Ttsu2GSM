# Reader2GSM Privicy Policy

This extension is open-source and operates entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, or see your data.

## Data Usage

  Reading Progress: Reader2GSM process book titles, text, and timestamps solely to track your progress.

  Local Storage: All history and settings are saved in your browser’s local database (IndexedDB/Storage). No data is synced to the cloud.

  Network: The extension only communicates with localhost. No external APIs are used.
